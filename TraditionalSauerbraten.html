Tradional Sauerbraten recipe

A tradtional German meal we make mainly on New Years Day. Its often serves with spetzal, potato dumplins and red cabbage

Ingredients:

    3 pounds beef rump roast
    2 large onions chopped
    1 cup vinegar
    1 Cup Water
    1 tablespoon salt
    1 tablespoon ground black pepper
    10 whole cloves
    2 bay leaves
    one small bag crumbled gingersnap cookies

Directions:

prep: 15 minutes

Cook 4 hours

Ready in 2 days 4 hours 15 minutes

  1. Place beef, onions, vinegar, water, salt, black pepper, cloves,and bay leaves in Large plastic bag to marinate then put all in a pot. Refigerate for 2-3 days. Remove from marinade, saving it for later.
  2. Cook beef for about 10 minutes until brown, then pour marinade on beef, cook it slow for about 3 hours, simmering until the beef is tender. Remove beef and slice on platter
  3. Strain solids from the remaining liquids and cook over medium heat, adding the gingersnaps and simmering until the gravy is thick. Serve over beef
